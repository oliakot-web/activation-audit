# Keystone

Keystone helps product teams evaluate and improve their user onboarding without running a full UX audit.

Upload screenshots of your onboarding flow, answer a few quick questions about your product and target users, and get a structured report based on the **Setup → Aha → Habit** framework — highlighting where users get confused, lose motivation, or drop off, with actionable recommendations and four key scores: Setup, Aha, Habit, and Overall Activation.

**Audience:** Product designers, PMs, founders, and growth teams at early-stage and mid-sized SaaS companies.

**Platform:** Responsive web app, desktop-first.

See [CLAUDE.md](./CLAUDE.md) for the full product brief.
